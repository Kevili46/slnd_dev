Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy informs about how slnd.dev collects, uses and shares your personal data.

1. Collected Information

There are several types of information that are collected from you when using this website:

  • Information You Provide Directly: This includes information you may provide when using certain features of this website, such as the content of the conversation when you interact with the chatbot. Do not share personal information.
  • Information Collected Automatically: When you access and use this website, we may automatically collect certain information to recognize you and about your website interactions. For this purpose this website uses cookies.

2. Use of Your Information

Your Information is used for various purposes, including to:

  • Provide and Improve this website: To operate, maintain, and improve the website, its features and content.
  • Provide Features: To offer and improve the LLM-powered chatbot feature. The contents of your chats are processed by the Google Gemini API.
  • Personalize your Experience: To personalize your experience on this website, such as by remembering your preferences and recognizing you as a returning user through cookies.
  • Analyze Website Usage: To understand how users interact with this website, analyze trends, and measure the effectiveness of the content and features using Google Analytics.

3. Cookies and Similar Technologies

This website uses cookies, which are small text files placed on your device, and similar technologies for various purposes, including:

  • Provide and Improve this website: To operate, maintain, and improve the website, its features and content.
  • Essential Cookies: These cookies are necessary for the basic functionality of this website and to enhance user experience by recognizing you with each visit.

4. Sharing Your Information

Your information is shared with third parties in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Your information is shared with third-party service providers who assist in operating this website, providing the chat feature (Google for the Gemini API) and conducting analytics (Google for Google Analytics). These service providers are contractually obligated to protect your information.
  • With Your Consent: Your information is only shared with third parties with your explicit consent.

5. Data Retention

Your personal information will be retained for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law. Chat transcripts may be retained for a certain period to improve the chat feature and for internal record-keeping. Cookie data is typically retained for the duration specified by the cookie.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information, such as the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict the processing of your information. If you wish to exercise these rights, you can use the contact information provided in the Impressum.

7. Security

This website takes reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure, and absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

8. Third-Party Links

This website contains links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party sites. Please review the privacy policies of those websites before providing any personal information.

9. Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or the privacy practices of this website, you can express them by using the contact information provided in the Impressum.
